Overview

Embark on a unique and refreshing coastal journey that combines the vibrant charm of Goa with the serene, untouched beauty of Lakshadweep. Begin your trip with classic sightseeing in North Goa, visiting famous beaches and heritage forts, followed by a relaxing overnight stay. The next leg takes you to the pristine Agatti Island in Lakshadweep, where turquoise waters, white-sand beaches, and thrilling marine adventures await. Explore Thinnakara Island, indulge in snorkeling, witness dolphins on a cruise, and enjoy a glass-bottom boat ride at Kalpitty Rocky Island. After soaking in the tranquil island life, return to Goa for a quick tour of South Goa's cultural and coastal gems before flying back home. This package is perfect for travelers seeking a balance of exploration, water activities, and pure tropical relaxation — all in one seamless itinerary.

Day Wise Itinerary

Day 1 – Goa

Meals: Lunch, Dinner

Title: Arrival in Goa – North Goa Exploration

Arrive at Goa Airport and proceed for sightseeing: Fort Aguada, Calangute Beach, Baga Beach, and Anjuna Beach. Check-in, relax, and enjoy your dinner.

Day 2 – Lakshadweep

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Title: Travel to Lakshadweep – Arrival at Agatti Island

Fly from Goa to Agatti Island. Guided tour of Agatti: Museum, Eastern Jetty, and Lagoon beach. Dinner and overnight stay at resort.

Day 3 – Lakshadweep

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Title: Thinnakara Island Excursion / Dolphin Cruise

Boat ride to Thinnakara Island. Snorkeling session. Alternate: Dolphin Cruise if weather doesn’t permit island visit.

Day 4 – Lakshadweep

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Title: Kalpitty Rocky Island Visit & Leisure

Water sports in morning (optional). Evening visit to Kalpitty Island. Glass-bottom boat ride to see marine life.

Day 5 – Goa

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch

Title: Return to Goa – South Goa Sightseeing – Departure

Return flight to Goa, quick South Goa sightseeing, and drop at Goa Airport for return flight to Hyderabad.
